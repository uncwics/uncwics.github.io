Our Mission

Women in Computer Science provides a community for women in the field of computer science and additional fields involving programming. WICS is primarily concerned with facilitating a fun, welcoming, and engaging environment for its club members. We also provided opportunities via networking with partnered organizations and companies. Women in Computer Science also aims to hold multiple workshops and seminars geared towards breaking into and navigating the tech world.
