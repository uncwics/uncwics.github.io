Women In Computer Science

Women in Computer Science at UNC provides a community for women in the field of computer science and additional fields involving programming. WICS is primarily concerned with providing opportunities to its club members via networking with partnered organizations and companies. We hold multiple workshops and seminars geared towards breaking into and navigating the tech world.
